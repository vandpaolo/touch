   phase-1 adapter implementation surface** that's about to begin.

   dedicated `/pm-architecture` polish pass after phase-1 closes**,
   when the actual adapter implementation may reshape which glossary
   terms matter and what their precise meanings are.

**Carry-forward to phase-1 report:** record this override as a known

`/pm-architecture` pass. Specifically: a glossary audit of
